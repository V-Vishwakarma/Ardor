ABOUT OUR ARCHITECTS

Sahil Sharma
(Founder and CEO)

LEADERSHIP

Make with love all what I do.

A Senior Architect turned Entrepreneur in 2016 - Architect Sahil Sharma (founder) Had been a very Diversified journey so far with the Best of experiences in the World of Architecture.

Sahil has previously worked with the biggest players of Indian Market. He says " It was my luck to be associated with my dream architects in my early career ". He started his journey by working for Hafeez Contractor and Rsp Bombay. I dealt into Hi-rise, Luxury Housing, landscape and luxury interiors while working in my initial career. The exposure and practical knowledge of the industry was a key stone in laying the foundation of Ardor.

Having pursued a bachelor’s from Apeejay School of Architecture and planning, Sahil was his college representative at various national/international platforms in his college days. Was elected President for NASA India, Northern Region in 2012.

Marketing, awareness, exposure & strong technical knowledge of the field have been major advantages to his profile that added up to his role here. Sahil is majorly involved in Design & expansion of the Studio along with setting quality benchmarks.

Our Vision, Mission & Commitments

Our Vision

At our core, we envision a future where architectural design is seamlessly integrated with nature and technology. Our goal is to create spaces that not only serve functional purposes but also inspire and elevate the human experience. We aim to push boundaries and set new standards in the industry through creativity and innovation.

Discover more about our vision and how we plan to achieve it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional architectural solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability. We are committed to understanding our clients' needs and translating them into innovative designs that are both practical and aesthetically pleasing. Our focus is on creating lasting value and building enduring relationships.

Our Commitments

We are dedicated to maintaining the highest levels of integrity, transparency, and professionalism in every project we undertake.
